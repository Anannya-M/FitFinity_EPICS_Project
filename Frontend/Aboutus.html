Who are we?

Hello everyone! Hope you all are doing well. We are 3rd students of Vellore institute of technology, Bhopal , We have made this platform under " Enginnering project in community service" (EPICS) under supervision of Dr.Pankaj Kumar . The main objective of EPICS project is to overcome basic issues of community and to help them to excel and grow in particular domain. We have created this platform to help the healthcare sector.

AIM

Fit - fitnity "Health and fitness based platform" Through our webapp Fit-Finity, people can increase their physical fitness and general health by using personalised exercise recommendations. Our webapp recommends specific exercises catering to the demands and objectives of each particular individual, thus making our applications more efficient and successful. Fit-Finity can be a valuable tool for individuals looking to improve their fitness and achieve their health goals. When individuals see progress and results from their exercise routine, this will encourage them to continue working towards their goals.
