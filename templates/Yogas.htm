It Improves circulation by bringing fresh blood flow to the brain and upper body.

It helps relieve stress and anxiety by promoting relaxation and deep breathing.

 

Yoga Pose 3 - Shoulder Stand

Shoulder stand is a yoga pose that involves supporting the body on the shoulders while keeping the neck and head straight.The benefits of shoulder stand include improved circulation, increased flexibility, strengthened neck and shoulder muscles, and reduced stress and anxiety. It can also stimulate the thyroid gland, which can help regulate metabolism and energy levels.

 

 

 

Dietary Habits

l  Biotin is a B-vitamin that helps to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. Foods that are rich in biotin include eggs, nuts and seeds, sweet potatoes, spinach, and broccoli.

l  Protein is essential for building and repairing tissues, including hair. Foods that are high in protein include lean meats, poultry, fish, beans, lentils, and soy products.

l  Iron and zinc are both important minerals for maintaining healthy hair growth. Iron helps to transport oxygen to hair follicles, while zinc helps to keep the oil glands around the hair follicles working properly. Foods that are rich in iron include red meat, poultry, fish, beans, and spinach. Foods that are high in zinc include oysters, beef, pork, chicken, beans, nuts, and whole grains.
